SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. It is the process of improving a website or web page to increase its visibility in search engine results pages (SERPs) for relevant keywords.

Definitions:
1. SEO is the practice of optimizing website content, structure, and technical aspects to rank higher in search engines like Google, Bing, or Yahoo.
2. SEO involves making changes to your website design and content to make it more attractive to search engines.
3. SEO is a digital marketing strategy focused on increasing the quantity and quality of traffic to your website through organic search engine results.

Examples:
- Using relevant keywords in your website content (e.g., "best Italian restaurant in New York").
- Optimizing meta tags (title, description) for each web page.
- Creating high-quality, original content that answers users' questions.
- Improving website loading speed and mobile-friendliness.
- Getting backlinks from reputable websites to increase your site's authority.
- Structuring your website with clear navigation and internal links.

In summary, SEO helps websites appear higher in search results, attracting more visitors without paying for ads.
